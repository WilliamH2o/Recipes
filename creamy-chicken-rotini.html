Creamy Chicken Rotini

A roux-based creamy pasta with shredded chicken, red bell pepper, zucchini, and parmesan.

Work in progress. This recipe is still being developed and may be incomplete or change.

Ingredients

Pasta and Protein

  • 16 oz rotini
  • 3 cups shredded chicken

Base

  • 2 tbsp bacon grease
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 red bell pepper (half diced, half sliced)
  • 1 tsp table salt
  • 0.5 tsp NoSalt
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 0.5 tsp white pepper

Roux

  • 3 tbsp avocado oil
  • 3 tbsp flour

Sauce

  • 1.5 cups water
  • 3 cups lactose-free milk
  • 0.5 cups lactose-free milk (warm, added with sauce mix)
  • 0.5 cups chicken bone broth
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 0.5 tsp lemon juice

Finishing

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 zucchini (medium-thin sliced and halved)
  • 3 green onion tops (scissor-chopped small)
  • 6 tbsp finely grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Boil the rotini and strain. Set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the warm milk, chicken bone broth, sugar, and lemon juice. Set aside.
  3. Heat the bacon grease and 2 tbsp butter in a large pot or very large sauté pan over medium heat. Add the red bell pepper, salt, NoSalt, parsley, paprika, and white pepper. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened. Push the vegetables to one side of the pot.
  4. Add the avocado oil to the cleared center of the pot. Whisk in the flour and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the roux smells nutty and no longer raw.
  5. Gradually whisk in the 3 cups milk and 1.5 cups water into the roux side, adding a splash at a time and whisking fully before adding more. Once all liquid is incorporated and smooth, stir the vegetables back in.
  6. Add the rotini, 2 tbsp butter, shredded chicken, and the sauce mix. Stir together well.
  7. Bring to a hard simmer over medium heat, stirring frequently, then reduce to a steady simmer. Stir in the green onion tops and zucchini and cook down, stirring several times, until the sauce coats the back of a spoon and holds a clean line when you drag a finger through it.
  8. Remove from heat. Fold in the parmesan until fully melted and incorporated.
