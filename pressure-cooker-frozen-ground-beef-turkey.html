Pressure Cooker Frozen Ground Beef or Turkey

Cooks a standard 1 lb frozen block of ground beef or turkey fully through in 20 minutes using a trivet, so the meat steams rather than boils.

Equipment

  • Pressure cooker or Instant Pot
  • Trivet (the rack that comes with most pressure cookers)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb frozen ground beef or ground turkey (straight from the freezer, still in a solid block)
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 cup of water into the pressure cooker pot.
  2. Place the trivet in the pot. The trivet keeps the meat elevated above the water so it steams rather than sits in liquid.
  3. Set the frozen block of ground meat on the trivet.
  4. Seal the lid and set the pressure valve to sealing.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 20 minutes if tube or tube cut, not flat.
  6. When the cycle ends, do a quick pressure release by switching the valve to venting.
  7. Remove the meat. It will be cooked through but still largely in one piece. Break it apart.
