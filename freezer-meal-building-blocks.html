Freezer Meal Building Blocks

A modular freezer block system where any random grab still produces a reasonable meal.

New blocks are only added if they work with everything already in rotation. The test is whether a random combination would still hold together as a meal. This rotation is approximately low-FODMAP and low-oxalate.

Equipment

  • Silicone freezer molds, 1/2 cup capacity (Soupercube or equivalent)

Building Blocks

  • 1/2 cup Rice
  • 2 Tbsp Lentils
  • 1 Tbsp Kale
  • 1/3 cup Shredded Chicken Breast
  • 1/4 cup Taco Meat
  • 1/2 cup Baby Carrots
  • 1/2 cup Macaroni and Cheese
  • 1 Tbsp Chicken Bone Broth

Serving

No sauces. Nine times out of ten, the bowl is calorie-boosted with 1 Tbsp avocado oil and 1/4 cup shredded cheese, added during the final stretch of reheating.

Reheating

Single blocks of rice, lentils, kale, and either meat reheat together in about 3 minutes 33 seconds.

  1. Place blocks in a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Microwave for 3 minutes 33 seconds.
  3. At the 33-second mark, add 1 Tbsp avocado oil and 1/4 cup shredded cheese.
  4. Let the remaining time finish, then serve.
