Ham & Cheese Sliders

Soft King's Hawaiian rolls packed with layers of deli ham and Swiss cheese, brushed with a buttery Dijon and Worcestershire sauce before baking.

High-FODMAP Low-Oxalate

Ingredients

Rolls and Filling

  • 12 King's Hawaiian dinner rolls
  • 9 oz deli ham, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz Swiss cheese, thinly sliced

Butter Sauce

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp dried onion
  • 1 Tbsp poppy seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Combine the butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and brown sugar in a small saucepan over low heat, stirring until the butter melts. Stir in the dried onion and remove from heat.
  3. Slice the rolls horizontally to open for sandwiches. Set the tops aside and arrange the bottoms in the prepared baking dish.
  4. Layer the ham evenly over the roll bottoms. Lay the Swiss cheese on top of the ham.
  5. Place the roll tops back on. Brush or pour the butter sauce evenly over the rolls. Sprinkle with poppy seeds if using.
  6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the rolls are lightly browned and the cheese has melted.
  7. Separate into individual sliders and serve warm.
