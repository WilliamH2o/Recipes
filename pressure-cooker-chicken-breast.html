Pressure Cooker Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast

A simple, reliable method for cooking chicken breast in the Instant Pot — season to taste, eight minutes, done.

Low-FODMAP Low-Oxalate

Equipment

  • Instant Pot or pressure cooker

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup water
  • Seasoning to taste

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 cup of water into the pressure cooker.
  2. Place the chicken breast on the pot's trivet and season to taste.
  3. Close the lid and set the valve to seal.
  4. Pressure cook on high for 8 minutes.
  5. When cooking ends, release the pressure.
  6. When the pin drops and all pressure is released, open the lid. The chicken is ready to serve or shred.
