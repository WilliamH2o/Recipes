Southern Green Beans

Tender green beans slow-cooked in chicken broth with bacon grease and aromatics until the liquid reduces to a rich, flavorful base.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (28 oz each) Allen's Italian Green Beans
  • 2 tablespoons bacon grease
  • 4 green onion tops
  • 32 oz chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Sauté the green onion tops and diced red bell pepper in bacon grease in a pot until softened.
  2. Add the chicken broth and butter, then bring to a boil.
  3. Add the kosher salt and white pepper.
  4. Reduce heat to simmer (setting 2.5) and cook for 2 to 3 hours, stirring often and scraping the sides/bottom as the liquid reduces.
  5. Add the green beans and reduce heat to low (setting 2).
  6. Continue cooking for 1 to 2 hours, stirring more frequently and scraping as the liquid continues to reduce.
