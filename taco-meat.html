Taco Meat

Used to season ground meat for a flavorful and low-FODMAP taco filling, mac and cheese, and pizza topping.

Low-FODMAP Low-Oxalate

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground meat (1 lb ground beef & 1 lb ground turkey)
  • 1+1/4 cup water
  • 1 Tbsp oil (bacon grease)
  • ~2–3 green onion tops (optional)

Seasoning Blend

  • 4 tsp cumin
  • 4 tsp ancho chili powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp salt
  • 1/2 Tbsp NoSalt
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano (Mexican)
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix the seasoning blend in a small bowl.
  2. Cook the ground meat in a skillet over medium-high heat until no longer pink.
  3. Drain the fat from the skillet.
  4. Add bacon grease to the hot skillet, then the seasoning blend, stir, then the green onion, then the cooked meat.
  5. Pour water into the skillet.
  6. Stir often as the mixture simmers until the liquid thickens into a light sauce that coats the meat.
