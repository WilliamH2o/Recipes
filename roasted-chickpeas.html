Roasted Chickpeas

Crispy oven-roasted chickpeas tossed in a parmesan seasoning.

High-FODMAP High-Oxalate

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas

Seasoning Mix

  • 1 + 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/8 tsp ancho chile powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
  2. Roll them dry on a bar towel, removing the skins.
  3. Spread the chickpeas on a parchment-lined quarter sheet pan.
  4. Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes.
  5. While they bake, mix the olive oil and all seasoning mix ingredients together.
  6. Roll the chickpeas around with a turner, or shake the pan.
  7. Continue baking for 15 minutes.
  8. Toss the chickpeas with the seasoning mix.
  9. Continue baking for another 15 minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven and rest for 10 minutes before eating or storing.
