Hamilton Beach Egg Cooker Water Amounts

Water measurement reference for the Hamilton Beach 25508 3-in-1 Egg Cooker, covering all three cooking modes: poached eggs, hard-boiled eggs, and omelets.

Notes

The manual does not recommend medium or soft doneness for 8–14 egg batches. Hard-boiled is the only listed doneness for those quantities.

Water Amounts

Mode Teaspoons Tablespoons + Teaspoons
Poached Hard 14 tsp 4 Tbsp + 2 tsp
Poached Med 10 tsp 3 Tbsp + 1 tsp
Poached Soft 8 tsp 2 Tbsp + 2 tsp
8 Eggs Hard 8 tsp 2 Tbsp + 2 tsp
10 Eggs Hard 7 tsp 2 Tbsp + 1 tsp
12 Eggs Hard 6 tsp 2 Tbsp
14 Eggs Hard 5 tsp 1 Tbsp + 2 tsp
1 Egg Omelet 6 tsp 2 Tbsp
2 Egg Omelet 10 tsp 3 Tbsp + 1 tsp
