Pink Wallop Energy Elixir

A watermelon-flavored energy soda made from a concentrated mix of Twist Up, Torani Watermelon Syrup, and dissolved caffeine tablets. The concentrate batches into 12 small bottles, each ready to top off with a can of soda.

Low-FODMAP Low-Oxalate

NOTE: Low-FODMAP assumes Zero Sugar Twist Up. Regular Twist up contains between 1/2-3/4 tsp of High Fructose Corn Syrup (HFCS-55) per ounce. HFCS-55: 1/4 tsp is low-FODMAP; 1/2 tsp is med-FODMAP. 3/4 tsp is high-FODMAP. This recipe uses 13 ounces of Twist up which would be 6.5 tsp of HFCS-55.

Equipment

  • Cocktail shaker or small jar with a tight lid
  • 12 glass bottles with lids, approximately 2 oz each

Ingredients

Concentrate (Makes 12 Bottles)

  • 12 oz Sugar Free Twist Up, from a 2-liter (1 oz per bottle)
  • 12 oz Torani Watermelon Syrup (1 oz per bottle)
  • 12 Equate Stay Awake Caffeine 200mg Tablets (1 per bottle)

To Serve (Per Drink)

  • 1/2–1 concentrate bottle (~1–2 oz; adjusts caffeine from 100mg to 200mg)
  • 12 oz Sugar Free Twist Up (from a can at work, or from a 2-liter at home)

Instructions

Batch Prep

  1. Crush 1 caffeine tablet into a fine powder.
  2. Combine the crushed tablet with 1 oz Twist Up and 1 oz Torani Watermelon Syrup in the shaker.
  3. Seal and shake until the tablet is fully dissolved.
  4. Pour into one glass bottle and seal.
  5. Repeat steps 1 through 4 for each remaining bottle until all 12 are filled.
  6. Store at room temperature or refrigerate.

To Serve

  1. Pour 1/2 to 1 concentrate bottle into a glass, depending on desired caffeine level.
  2. Add 12 oz Twist Up and stir gently to combine.

Notes

Dosing Safety

Mix each bottle individually rather than dissolving all 12 tablets in one large batch. Individual mixing ensures each bottle contains exactly one tablet's worth of caffeine and prevents uneven distribution across bottles.

Cost per Drink

At work using a can of Twist Up: approximately $0.76 to $0.79 per drink. At home using a 2-liter: approximately $0.54 to $0.58 per drink.

Caffeine

Each concentrate bottle contains one Equate Stay Awake Caffeine 200mg Tablet. Using a full bottle yields 200mg per drink; using half yields 100mg. For context: a standard 8.4 oz Red Bull has 80mg, a 16 oz Rockstar Original has 160mg, a 16 oz Rip It has 160mg, and a 12 oz Celsius has 200mg — putting a full-bottle Pink Wallop squarely in the Celsius tier. Do not exceed your personal caffeine tolerance.

There is no reliable way to “flush out” caffeine fast. The main fix is time and caffeine takes 3–10 hours to break down.

Consuming protein/carbs, like sourdough toast with peanut butter, yogurt, eggs, rice, oatmeal, less-ripe banana, or crackers can help reduce nausea/shakiness.
