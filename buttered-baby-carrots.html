Pressure Cooker Buttered Baby Carrots

low-FODMAP, tender baby carrots cooked quickly under pressure and finished in a buttery herb seasoning.

Low-FODMAP Low-oxalate

Equipment

  • Pressure cooker

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 4 Tbsp butter, salted or unsalted
  • 2 lb baby carrots

Seasoning Blend

  • 1 Tbsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 Tbsp dried dill
  • 2–4 Tbsp green onion tops
  • 1 tsp salt or kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix the seasoning blend in a small bowl. Rinse the baby carrots.
  2. Add the baby carrots, butter, and water to the pressure cooker.
  3. Sprinkle the seasoning blend evenly over the carrots.
  4. Close the pressure lid and set the valve to seal. Pressure cook on high for 3 minutes (Al Dente) 4 minutes (Soft).
  5. When cooking ends, immediately release pressure by moving the valve to vent.
  6. When the pin drops and all pressure is released, open the lid and stir the carrots in the butter and cooking liquid.
