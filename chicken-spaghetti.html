Chicken Spaghetti

A hearty baked pasta casserole with shredded chicken, sautéed aromatics, and melted Velveeta.

High-FODMAP Med-Oxalate

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp bacon grease
  • 1 Tbsp butter, or margarine
  • garlic powder (to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 lb cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 8 oz cubed Velveeta (or Melt 'n Dip)
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 cups diced white onion (about 1 medium onion)
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper (about 1/2 medium pepper)
  • 3/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • grated Parmesan cheese (1/4 cup for the casserole, plus more for topping)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain but do not rinse.
  2. Melt bacon grease and butter together in a skillet over medium-low heat (setting ~3.5). Sprinkle generously with garlic powder.
  3. Add onion, bell pepper, and mushrooms. Cook for 10 to 15 minutes until softened, then transfer to a large mixing bowl.
  4. Add cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, shredded chicken, salt, pepper, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and 1/4 cup Parmesan to the bowl. Stir until well combined.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti and stir until fully incorporated.
  6. Pour mixture into a greased 9×13 baking pan.
  7. Press cubed Velveeta in rows into the mixture, spacing evenly.
  8. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350°F for 18 minutes.
  10. Remove foil and stir. Bake uncovered for an additional 17 minutes.
  11. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

Tomatoes don't go in chicken spaghetti!
